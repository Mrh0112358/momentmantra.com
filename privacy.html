Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Welcome to Moment Mantra ("we", "our", or "us"), accessible at www.momentmantra.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our website operates. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

By using Moment Mantra you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the site.

Section 01

Who We Are

Moment Mantra is a mindfulness web application that generates personalised mantras based on words entered by the visitor. The site is operated as an independent website based in the United Kingdom.

For any privacy-related queries please contact us at: hello@momentmantra.com

Section 02

What Information We Collect

Moment Mantra is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not require you to create an account, register, or provide any personal information to use the service.

Words you enter: The three words you type to describe your feelings are sent to the Claude AI API to generate your mantra. These words are processed in real time and are not stored, logged, or retained by us after your mantra has been generated.

Automatically collected data: Like most websites, certain technical information may be automatically collected when you visit, including your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and time of visit. This data is collected via third-party services described below and is used in aggregate, anonymised form only.

We use the following third-party services to operate Moment Mantra:

Section 06

Your Rights Under GDPR

As we are based in the United Kingdom and may serve visitors from the European Economic Area, we are committed to upholding your rights under the UK GDPR and EU GDPR. These rights include:

The right to access — you have the right to request information about any personal data we hold about you.

The right to erasure — you have the right to request that we delete any personal data we hold about you.

The right to restrict processing — you have the right to request that we limit how we use your data.

The right to object — you have the right to object to our processing of your personal data.

The right to data portability — you have the right to request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

As Moment Mantra does not collect or store personal data beyond what is described in this policy, most of these rights are satisfied by design. To exercise any of these rights please contact us using the details below.
